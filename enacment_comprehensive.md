### Agile Methodology

Agile is an iterative approach to project management and software development that helps teams deliver value faster. It emphasizes continuous evaluation of requirements, plans, and results, allowing for quick responses to change. Key principles include:

*   **Individuals and interactions** over processes and tools.
*   **Working software** over comprehensive documentation.
*   **Customer collaboration** over contract negotiation.
*   **Responding to change** over following a plan.

We prioritize satisfying the client through early and continuous delivery of valuable software, with frequent deliveries (weeks to months). We welcome change, even late in development, to leverage competitive advantage. Client and developers work together daily, and face-to-face conversation is the most efficient way to transmit information.

### Design Thinking

Design Thinking is a problem-solving methodology that focuses on understanding users, identifying their needs, and generating innovative and effective solutions that create value for both the user and the business. It's a human-centered, collaborative, iterative, and prototype-based approach. The process involves:

1.  **Empathize:** Understand user needs, pains, and expectations.
2.  **Define:** Analyze and prioritize problems to focus on those offering the most value.
3.  **Ideate:** Propose and select the best, most viable, and convenient solutions.
4.  **Prototype:** Create quick and inexpensive versions of solutions for testing.
5.  **Evaluate:** Test and validate prototypes, making necessary modifications before implementation.

This iterative process ensures continuous improvement and adaptation.
